If you have experienced online grooming, **you are not alone.** Many survivors struggle with feelings of shame, confusion, or isolation, but support is available.

**The Grooming Files is not a crisis service.** If you are in immediate danger or need urgent help, please reach out to professional support services listed below.

---

## **Wales-Specific Support Services**

**NSPCC Cymru/Wales**
The NSPCC works to prevent child abuse in Wales and supports affected children and young people.

- **Website:** [NSPCC Wales](https://www.nspcc.org.uk/about-us/what-we-do/wales/)

**Lucy Faithfull Foundation Wales**
A child protection charity dedicated to preventing child sexual abuse across Wales.

- **Website:** [Lucy Faithfull Foundation Wales](https://www.lucyfaithfull.org.uk/wales/)

**Barnardo's Wales**
Provides support for vulnerable children and their families, including specialist services for those affected by exploitation.

- **Website:** [Barnardo's Wales](https://www.barnardos.org.uk/get-support/support-for-parents-and-carers/child-abuse-and-harm/child-sexual-abuse-and-exploitation)

**South Wales Police – Online Grooming Advice**
Offers information on recognising and reporting grooming incidents.

- **Website:** [South Wales Police Grooming Advice](https://www.south-wales.police.uk/advice/advice-and-information/gr/grooming/)

**Hwb – Online Safety Resources**
A digital platform providing resources about staying safe online and addressing concerns about grooming.

- **Website:** [Hwb Online Grooming](https://hwb.gov.wales/keeping-safe-online/online-grooming)

**Powys Child Exploitation Team**
Offers advice and guidance on child criminal and sexual exploitation in the Powys region.

- **Email:** [child.exploitation@powys.gov.uk](mailto:child.exploitation@powys.gov.uk)

- **Phone:** 01597 827666 (for safeguarding concerns)

- **Website:** [Powys Child Exploitation Support](https://en.powys.gov.uk/article/9127/Child-Exploitation-support-for-children-young-people-and-families)

## **NAPAC – Support for Adult Survivors of Childhood Abuse**

**NAPAC provides free support to adults who suffered abuse in childhood, including survivors of grooming and exploitation.** Their services are **confidential, trauma-informed, and available across Wales and the UK.**

- **Helpline:** 0808 801 0331 (Free & confidential)

- **Website:** [www.napac.org.uk](https://www.napac.org.uk)

- **Email:** [support@napac.org.uk](mailto:support@napac.org.uk)

**They offer practical guidance, emotional support, and referrals to specialist services.**

---

## **Helping Survivors – Rideshare Sexual Assault Support**

Helping Survivors is an advocacy organisation offering guidance and resources to those impacted by sexual violence, including survivors of rideshare-related assaults. Their platform provides survivor-led information, legal rights guidance, and emotional support content for those affected.

Their services are accessible, informative, and available across the UK and beyond.

**Website:** [helpingsurvivors.org/rideshare-sexual-assault](https://helpingsurvivors.org/rideshare-sexual-assault)

**Email:** [pr@helpingsurvivors.org](mailto:pr@helpingsurvivors.org)

**They offer guidance on legal options, trauma resources, and connections to additional survivor support.**
